### 4. Hybrid physical/logical zero-noise extrapolation with limited logical executions

### 5. Fixed Point Exploration For CV-QKD IR QC-MET-LDPC Toward Hardware Implementation

**来源**: arXiv quant-ph  
**时间**: 刚刚  
**链接**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17960
