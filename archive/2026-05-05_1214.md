### 3. Non-linear Sigma Model for the Surface Code with Coherent Errors

**来源**: arXiv quant-ph  
**时间**: 刚刚  
**链接**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25665

### 5. Fault-tolerant interfaces for modular quantum computing on diverse qubit platforms
